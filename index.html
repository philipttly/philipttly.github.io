Bio

I am a product leader dedicated to solving customer pain points through a customer-centric and data-driven approach. By deeply understanding customer needs, I focus on identifying challenges and creating solutions that deliver meaningful value and improve the overall product experience. My work often involves aligning products with specialized workflows, ensuring they not only meet functional requirements but also seamlessly integrate into the lives of the people who use them.

In the life sciences sector, I’ve been involved in numerous product launches, particularly for private label and distributed products. I leverage data and analytics to guide strategies, shape value propositions, and drive successful product introductions. By thinking through the customer journey, I aim to challenge assumptions and uncover opportunities to enhance usability, efficiency, and impact.

Collaborating with cross-functional teams, I strive to deliver products that are thoughtful, impactful, and purpose-driven. Whether it’s developing a go-to-market strategy or addressing gaps in the customer journey, my goal is to ensure that every product I work on addresses real needs and provides lasting value.

Noteworthy Skills

I bring a unique blend of skills to product management, combining a deep understanding of the product lifecycle with years of experience in project management and data-driven decision-making. From customer interviews and journey mapping to hypothesis validation and data analysis, I take a holistic approach to understanding user needs and aligning products to meet them. This foundation allows me to craft strategies that add meaningful value for customers while addressing their pain points.

My experience spans both research and business-focused projects, including risk management initiatives and product launches. Along the way, I’ve honed my expertise in marketing approaches, UX principles, and even AI tools, leveraging these to create products that not only meet functional requirements but also resonate with users. Whether using tools like Notion, Google Sheets, Tableau, Salesforce, or PowerBI, I rely on data to uncover insights, validate ideas, and make informed decisions that drive growth.

What sets me apart is my ability to blend technical expertise with a customer-first perspective. I thrive on connecting the dots between user needs, market dynamics, and product functionality to deliver solutions that are impactful and purpose-driven. This thoughtful, holistic approach to product management ensures that every product I work on is well-positioned to succeed while creating a lasting, positive impact for the people who use it.
